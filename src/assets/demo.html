Stripes

Stripes are called external letter structures that set the background and styles beyond the boundaries of the letter.

What should we use stripes for?
  • To create whole email background;
  • To expand the banner;
  • To highlight the content using the stripe color.

Structures

The structure is the bar area where you post content. It is located between the side borders of the email.

Use ready-made structure or create your own directly in the settings.

Save the structure in your own library of modules and use it in any email simply by dragging it from the library.

Blocks

Using blocks, you can fill out the content of the email. In addition to the basic blocks, interactive blocks are also available in Stripo, such as a countdown timer, an HTML block, and even an AMP carousel.
